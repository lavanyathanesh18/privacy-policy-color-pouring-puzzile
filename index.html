Privacy Policy

Color Pouring Sort Puzzle  •  Last updated: [20/07/2026]

This Privacy Policy explains how Color Pouring Sort Puzzle ("the app," "we," "us") handles information when you play. Color Pouring Sort Puzzle is a single-player puzzle game and does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to play.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information stored only on your device

Your game progress — including coins, stars, unlocked levels, selected character, theme, and settings — is stored locally on your device only. This data is never transmitted to us and we never see it. Uninstalling the app deletes it.

1.2 Information collected by third-party services

The app uses Google AdMob to show ads (banner, interstitial, and rewarded-video ads) that support free play. AdMob may collect and process:

Data typePurpose
Advertising IDAd delivery and (for eligible users) ad personalization
Device information (model, OS version)Ad compatibility and fraud prevention
Approximate location (derived from IP)Regionally relevant ads and legal compliance
App interaction data (ad impressions/clicks)Ad performance measurement

This data is collected and processed by Google, not by us directly. See Google's own policies for details:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses information from AdMob

2. Children's Privacy & Mixed-Audience Notice

Color Pouring Sort Puzzle is a casual puzzle game that can appeal to players of a wide range of ages, including some players under 13. In accordance with Google Play Families policies and the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children.
  • Where required, the app requests non-personalized ads only for users identified as children, meaning no behavioral ad targeting or ad personalization occurs for those sessions.
  • The app does not include external links, chat, social features, or in-app purchases directed at children beyond the level/coin system described below.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us using the details below and we will take appropriate action.

3. In-App Purchases & Virtual Currency

The app includes an in-game coin system earned through gameplay, and (if enabled) optional purchases of coin packs or an "remove ads" upgrade through the Google Play billing system. Payment information is handled entirely by Google Play and is never seen or stored by us.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your information. We do not share any information with third parties except as necessary for the third-party services described above (AdMob) to function, and as required by law.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Locally stored game data persists on your device until you uninstall the app or clear its storage in your device settings. We do not hold a server-side copy of this data. For data collected by AdMob, refer to Google's retention practices in the policies linked above; you can also reset your device's advertising ID or opt out of ad personalization in your device's ad settings at any time.

6. Your Choices

  • Reset progress: use the "Reset Progress" option in the app's Settings screen.
  • Limit ad tracking: adjust your device's ad personalization settings (Android: Settings → Google → Ads; iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking).
  • Uninstall: removes all locally stored app data from your device.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
